## The Job

A little over a month ago, my LinkedIn profile came to the attention of the person to
whom I now report at my fantastic new job. He was looking for someone a new developer
for his team, someone with a passion for JavaScript, mapping, and public health. The
intersection of my skills and interests make me a perfect fit for the gig, and I'm
feeling so grateful every day that I get to sling code for a worthy cause with lovely
people who embrace learning and doing good with data as a life's mission.
