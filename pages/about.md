* My name is Tree. My birth name is Theresa, but if you say it quickly, it sounds like Tree-sa, hence the nickname. My parents have been calling me Tree my whole life.

* I adore writing, teaching, and playing music. It's what gets me up in the morning. I used to teach piano for a living. I hope to have a home studio again soon so I can teach one or two students each week for the joy of it. I am currently focusing on classical guitar and bluegrass mandolin. I really want a nyckelharpa.

* I'm passionate about all things geospatial. I love cartography and
geographic information systems and could spend hours with Google Earth or a good
old-fashioned atlas. I just applied to a Master of Science in Cartography and GIS
Development program. If I am accepted, my dream of combining my love of code with my love
 of maps will come to pass.

* I love languages. I studied French for five years in my teens and am kicking myself for not majoring in Linguistics (and Computer Science) instead of Psychology. I am currently learning Turkish, Italian, and Icelandic, and I know a little German. I wish I didn't need sleep so I could study Welsh, Estonian, and Basque (and learn the cello!). If I were rich and could do wildly impractical things, I would get my PhD in historical linguistics. For fun.

* Weird fact about me - when I was little, I would draw intricate maps of places found in the fantasy fiction I would try to write. I also made up languages to go with these places. I cared more about the places and their strange grammars than I did about plot and character development. I'm not sure what this says about me, but if you have done similar things, let me know. We would probably become fast friends.

* I like animals far better than most people. I believe that if you don't have a heart for animals, you don't have a heart at all.
